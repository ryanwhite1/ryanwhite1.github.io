My Annotated Papers

Like many other astrophysicists, I draw all over my academic papers to make them more accessible for a non-expert audience. You can see my annotated papers here!

The annotated summary is the wonderful idea of Dr Claire Lamman, where us scientists scribble on and annotate our technical, jargon-filled papers in an effort to make it more accessible for the general reader or non-expert. I have written an astrobites article on this exact idea, which you can access here, so I won't belabour the point on this page.
