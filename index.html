Hey there!
I'm Ryan.

I'm a Master of Research student at Macquarie University
I like to study stars, galaxies, and everything in between

I am currently studying Wolf-Rayet stars in binary systems as the core focus of my Honours project. I'm interested in a broad range of astrophysics (not just stars!) and have done projects looking at time dilation with supernovae, and merging binary black holes around active galactic nuclei.
I graduated from the University of Queensland in 2023 with a Bachelor of Science (Physics) and a Bachelor of Mathematics (Applied Mathematics). That is also where I work as a teaching assistant, and I'm passionate about helping out students and developing engaging course material.
