My hobby projects


I do get up to some things here and there outside of work and I'd love to tell you about them (you did click on this page, after all).


Delicious, delicious baking

I was a little late to the sourdough party from COVID, but I feel like I've gone deeper down the sourdough rabbit hole than most. Whenever I'm working from home, I always have a loaf proofing in the kitchen behind me and it's become something of an unhealthy obsession. I promise I'm not trying to sell it to you but there's truly a lot of comfort in a freshly baked loaf of bread that you've made -- I'd encourage everyone to give it a try! Shoot me an email if you're curious about how to get started.

Aside from bread, I am alarmingly fond of baking sweet treats, be it cookies, cinnamon rolls, pies, or more. There's nothing better than making your entire house smell like baked goods.



Photography (when I can)

As I matured as a landscape artist, I found more and more that I wanted to get outside to photograph more beautiful scenery across Australia. I don't count myself as a professional by any respect, but I do really enjoy taking photos of natural scenes and I try to get out to do so any chance I get.
